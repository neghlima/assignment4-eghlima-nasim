Favorite Food

I love Pizza! Its my absolute favorite food because it brings back happy memories and is also so tasty. As far back as I can remember pizza was always something I had on Fridays with my family, my father would make it as an end of the week treat. In my 20's I visited Milan, Italy and my love for pizza grew even more after tasting authentic homemade pizza at a friends house.
